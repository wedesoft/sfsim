Hello everyone,

I released version 0.11-2 of [sfsim][1].
*sfsim* is a realistic 3D space flight simulator with an advanced single-stage-to-orbit space craft.

I have now added several sound effects and a music track:
* Added Andrewkn's [At the Edge of Space][4] for the event when crossing 100 kilometer height
* Air flow noise depending on dynamic pressure
* Drag noise caused by air brakes and gear
* Sound effects when retracting or deploying gear
* Main rocket engine sound
* Sonic boom
* RCS thruster sounds
* Tyre skiding sound

Let me know any feedback and comments in the [sfsim playtest discussion forum][2].

Enjoy!

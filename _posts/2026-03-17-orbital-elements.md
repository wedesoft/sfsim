Hello spacers,

I released version 0.15-1 of [sfsim][1].
*sfsim* is a realistic 3D space flight simulator with an advanced single-stage-to-orbit space craft.

This version displays some orbital information:
* height of periapsis above surface
* height of apoapsis above surface
* time to periapsis
* time to apoapsis

This should make it much easier to achieve a stable orbit.
For example if the periapsis is moving closer (or even was passed) in the later stages of a launch, you need to pitch up to prevent the spacecraft from dropping.

Also Andrewkn's [Surrealism (Ambient Mix)][3] is played when achieving low Earth orbit (both periapsis and apoapsis above 160 kilometer).

Let me know any feedback and comments in the [sfsim playtest discussion forum][2].

Enjoy!

Hi,

Here is release version 0.23-1 of [sfsim][1].

![Steam Achievements](/sfsim/pics/achievements.jpg)

I started adding achievements to *sfsim*.

To begin with I added the following achievements:
* breaking the sound barrier: spacecraft exceeds speed of sound
* crossing the Kármán line: spacecraft reaches altitude of more than 100 kilometers
* achieving stable Earth orbit: lowest point of orbit is above 160 kilometers

I think achievements are a useful feature of Steam, because they give feedback to players about their progress in the game.

Let me know in the [sfsim playtest discussion forum][2] if you have any thoughts.
Don't forget to [wishlist sfsim][3]!

Enjoy!

Hi,

I have released version 0.20-1 of [sfsim][1].

*sfsim* now has an orbit display to make launching into an orbit easier.
The orbit display shows a visualisation of the planet Earth and the orbit as well as several orbital parameters:
* periapsis altitude
* apoapsis altitude
* current altitude
* eccentricity
* orbital period
* time to periapsis
* time to apoapsis
* velocity
* inclination
* longitude of ascending node

![Orbit MFD](/sfsim/pics/orbit-mfd.jpg)

Let me know any feedback and comments in the [sfsim playtest discussion forum][2].

Enjoy!
